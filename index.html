F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much does it cost?

    The cost for our spring session is $1700. Covering 6 tournaments, Jerseys, and 2 practices a week. Travel and Stay is not included and will be covered by the player's family. For more questions please contact us at: hardtoguardhoops@gmail.com.

  • Do you offer any scholarships?

    Yes we offer scholarships opportunities to those who are in need and qualify. Here at H2G we believe all players should have the opportunity to compete and develop their basketball game, regardless, of their financial background. Please email us for more information about this at: hardtoguardhoops@gmail.com.

  • When should I pay?

    Please bring check to first practice and give it to your head coach. Make the check out to ClubPrototype. If you would like to make other types of payments please email us at: hardtoguardhoops@gmail.com.

  • Are there any forms I need to fill out?

    Yes, please fill out the registration and release form by clicking here.
